About Me

I am a Mechatronics Student at Interdisciplinary Professional Unit in Engineering and Advanced Technologies UPIITA IPN, with an academic excellence scholarship, expected to graduate in January 2024. I have a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Mechatronics Design, Electronics, Research, CAE Simulation, CAD Design, Cooperative Robotics, Artificial Vision, and Autonomous Robotics. I have participated in multiple contests and projects related to these fields, such as NASA Space Challenge, CanSat CUCEI 2021 (First place), CanSat Competition 2022 (14th place) and Rover University Challenge 2022 (15th). I am also a Lead at Google Developer Student Club IPN - UPIITA, where I organize and facilitate events, workshops, and hackathons to promote the learning of new technologies and create networking opportunities for students
